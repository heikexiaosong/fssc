#### 【定时器】 电子回单匹配业务单号

- cn.ztessc.service.task.JobCenter#synEleMatchBoe

```sql
-- 待处理数据
select *
from FMS_ELECTRONIC_RECEIPT
where ENABLED_FLAG = '0'
  and (BOE_MATCH_MARK <> '1' or BOE_MATCH_MARK is null)
  and (BOE_MATCH_TIME <= 50 or BOE_MATCH_TIME is null)
order by BOE_MATCH_TIME asc, CREATE_DATE desc
```
